grover cleveland the first democrat elected after the civil war grover cleveland wa the only president to leave the white house and return for a second term four year later one of nine child of a presbyterian minister cleveland wa born in new jersey in 1837 he wa raised in upstate new york as a lawyer in buffalo he became notable for his single-minded concentration upon whatever task faced him at 44 he emerged into a political prominence that carried him to the white house in three year running a a reformer he wa elected mayor of buffalo in 1881 and later governor of new york cleveland won the presidency with the combined support of democrats and reform republicans the mugwumps who disliked the record of his opponent james g blaine of maine a bachelor cleveland wa ill at ease at first with all the comfort of the white house i must go to dinner he wrote a friend but i wish it wa to eat a pickled herring a swiss cheese and a chop at louis' instead of the french stuff i shall find in june 1886 cleveland married 21-year-old frances folsom; he wa the only president married in the white house cleveland vigorously pursued a policy barring special favor to any economic group vetoing a bill to appropriate $10 000 to distribute seed grain among drought-stricken farmer in texas he wrote federal aid in such case encourages the expectation of paternal care on the part of the government and weakens the sturdiness of our national character he also vetoed many private pension bill to civil war veteran whose claim were fraudulent when congress pressured by the grand army of the republic passed a bill granting pension for disability not caused by military service cleveland vetoed it too he angered the railroad by ordering an investigation of western land they held by government grant he forced them to return 81 000 000 acre he also signed the interstate commerce act the first law attempting federal regulation of the railroad in december 1887 he called on congress to reduce high protective tariff told that he had given republicans an effective issue for the campaign of 1888 he retorted what is the use of being elected or re-elected unless you stand for something? but cleveland wa defeated in 1888; although he won a larger popular majority than the republican candidate benjamin harrison he received fewer electoral vote elected again in 1892 cleveland faced an acute depression he dealt directly with the treasury crisis rather than with business failure farm mortgage foreclosure and unemployment he obtained repeal of the mildly inflationary sherman silver purchase act and with the aid of wall street maintained the treasury's gold reserve when railroad striker in chicago violated an injunction cleveland sent federal troop to enforce it if it take the entire army and navy of the united states to deliver a post card in chicago he thundered that card will be delivered cleveland's blunt treatment of the railroad striker stirred the pride of many americans so did the vigorous way in which he forced great britain to accept arbitration of a disputed boundary in venezuela but his policy during the depression were generally unpopular his party deserted him and nominated william jennings bryan in 1896 after leaving the white house cleveland lived in retirement in princeton new jersey he died in 1908
